Rooms & Ameneties

Captain's Cabin

Captain’s Cabins are approximately 250+ square feet, each with a king bed and an en-suite bathroom. All feature either Hudson River views or a private terrace.

Standard Cabin

All Cabins include super-fast wifi, flat-screen TVs, and luxurious linens. We offer bathroom amenities from C.O. Bigelow, NYC’s oldest apothecary.

Bunk Bed Cabin

Bunk Bed Cabins, at approximately 50 square feet, are reminiscent of a luxury ship’s cabin with storage below the bed.

Rooftop Terrace

The octagonal room was originally built as an observatory to be used as a navigation school. The terrace now overlooks views of the Husdson River.

Ballroom Bar

Located inside a grand ballroom, complete with mismatched couches, an oversize fireplace, and mezzanine with booths tucked under a disco ball.
